## What ThreadCloud is for

ThreadCloud is the buying operating system for the store. It does five things, in this order:

<ol class="steps">
  <li>
    <strong>Build an order</strong> — upload a supplier linesheet, AI extracts every style, size, colour, and price into a clean grid you can review and edit.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Enrich</strong> — descriptions, fit notes, fabric, country of origin, occasion, and 60+ Shopify metafields filled in by AI and approved by you.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Push to Shopify</strong> — products, variants, images, metafields, tags, and barcodes written in one batch. The storefront is live as soon as you approve.
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Receive</strong> — when the shipment arrives, mark received quantities, lock the FX rate, and print labels (Dymo or fallback Avery).
  </li>
  <li>
    <strong>Reorder & trade</strong> — sell-through, age, weeks-of-supply, brand scorecards. Re-buy what's working, mark down what isn't.
  </li>
</ol>
